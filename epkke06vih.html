Objective 2: Determine lethal and sublethal effects of treated and un-treated stormwater on mussels

Stormwater will be collected from stormwater flumes during storms and half of the samplestorms that do, and do not set off alarms and stored in amber glass bottles frozen at -20C. Frozen stormwater has been shown to retain its toxicity for at least 2 months following collection \citep{mcintyre_zebrafish_2014}. Within 2 months of collection, samples will be thawed and mussels exposed to untreated and treated storm water to determine the degree to which treatment ameliorates lethal and sublethal effects.
