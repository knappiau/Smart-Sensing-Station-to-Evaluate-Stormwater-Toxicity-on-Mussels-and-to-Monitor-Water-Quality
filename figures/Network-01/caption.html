(A) A map of Parkerson Mill creek watershed on Auburn University campus. The location for the Smart Sensing Station (objective 3) is shown in yellow. Red circles indicate locations of other stormwater flumes were further Smart Sensing Stations could be implemented. A schematic of a Smart Sensing Station network is shown in (B). Time series data from several locations in the watershed will allow to understand the contaminant dynamics in a urban stream. Shifts in time series data will be analyzed with wavelets (C).
